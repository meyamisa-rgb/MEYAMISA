The Human Typography

Movements of the body are her typography. Can you read through it? The body is always in motion, a breathing language that speaks before words appear.

Each gesture reveals what language often hides: hesitation, resistance, and desire. This project leaves strict rules behind and follows traces that stay in your mind.

The brain speaks in its own way, beyond awareness. Here, body language becomes type, and emotion transforms into asemic writing.

There was no fixed plan, only instinct, drawing, and feeling. A reminder that body and soul, movement and meaning, remain connected.
