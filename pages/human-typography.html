Plakat, Video, Experiment

The Human Typography

Movements of the body are her typography-can you read through it? The body is always in motion, a breathing form of language. Every gesture reveals what words cannot: hidden intentions, unspoken emotions. While we try to express ourselves through speech and writing, the body already communicates.

This project leaves rules behind to create traces, something that stays in your mind, something that makes you question what you see.

The brain speaks in its own way, beyond our awareness. It develops a language we don't fully understand. Here, body language becomes type, and emotion transforms into asemic writing.

There was no fixed plan-only an instinct, a drawing, a feeling to follow. A reminder that everything is connected: body and soul, movement and meaning.

My identity type premise: Movements of the body are her typography, can you read through it?

The brain: The human typography communicates from the inside.
