Exhibitions

Research-driven exhibition work, structured as an index.

Archive of installations, movement-based typography, and visual language experiments shown across Japan, South Korea, and Germany.

Kyoto, Japan

Studio Kan — Deviations

December 2024 · Curated by Chikako Yamazaki

Deviations presents alternative language systems through asemic writing, body traces, and layered visual structures. The exhibition focused on how typography can function as an emotional and spatial medium instead of fixed reading.

Tokyo, Japan

PUNIO Gallery — TSUKUMO

September 2023 · Solo exhibition

TSUKUMO explored the relationship between movement, memory, and typographic traces through installation sheets, spatial composition, and performative marks. The format combined still work, process material, and time-based visual experiments.

South Korea

Gallery Geoje — Seomkkot / GIAF

2025 · International presentation

Selected deviation works were presented in two contexts: gallery installation and art fair format. The project examined how body-derived symbols adapt across curation environments while preserving their intensity and ambiguity.

Aachen, Germany

Raststatte Aachen

2025 · Installation and live context

A presentation of graphic and gestural language in a public-facing exhibition setup. The installation emphasized process visibility, performance residue, and the translation of movement into visual form.
