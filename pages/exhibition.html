Studio KAN

Deviations

Deviations explores deviation as both disruption and creation through asemic writing, body language, and motion. This presentation gathers documentation, artworks, and media placeholders in one clear flow.

This exhibition was displayed between 2024 and 2025 in Japan, South Korea, and Germany.
