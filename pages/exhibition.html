Exhibition Titles

DEVIATIONS THE ART OF WRITING

Deviations - The art of writing solo exhibition at Studio Kan in Kyoto

Departing from the known, we invade the system. This rebellious act marks the first steps toward change. In this exhibition, we explore the act of deviation as an intentional challenge to established norms and a push against boundaries.

Through my artwork, you encounter crafts and techniques that illustrate writing systems, human emotions, and body expressions.

We all experience moments when peace is disrupted and transformed. This disturbance ripples outward, affecting those around us. Isn't that what we need?
