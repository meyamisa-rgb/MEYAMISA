Exhibition

Deviations

Studio Kan

Deviations is an exhibition by Mey Amisa that explores the concept of deviation as both disruption and creation. Through asemic writing, body language, and motion, the exhibition investigates how systems of language, emotion, and identity can be reimagined and reshaped.

It invites viewers to embrace deviation as a personal and collective act, using art to break boundaries, challenge norms, and foster new understandings.

This Exhibition was displayed between 2024 - 2025 in Japan, South Korea and Germany.
