Deviations - The art of writing

Curated by Chikako Yamazaki

DEVIATIONS SOLO EXHIBITION BY MEY AMISA

Departing from what is known, I move into the system as an intentional act of deviation. This exhibition follows the first movements of change, where writing becomes resistance and form becomes a way to challenge established norms.

Through these works, different crafts and gestures come together: writing systems, emotional states, and bodily expression. Each piece traces how language can be disrupted, rebuilt, and felt physically.

We all know moments when inner balance is interrupted. Something enters, shifts us, and transforms how we act. That disturbance moves outward and touches others around us.

Isn't that exactly what we need?
