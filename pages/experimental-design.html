Amygdala — Body as Archive

Experimental design installation · Nonverbal communication systems

Amygdala body as archive explores an alternative communication system for emotional truth in dysregulated states. Through movement, rhythm, and visual abstraction, the project translates preverbal feelings into embodied typographic language.

“The Amygdala reigns, control if its ever ours, we are the last ones to know.”
