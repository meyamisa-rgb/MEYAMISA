Folding Type

Henshin Gallery

Project

Supervision: Prof. Holger Jacobs

Project: Murosweek, Folding Type

Henshin is a performative design project where the body becomes typography. Developed during an intensive week in Intramuros, it explores folding as a method of transformation through movement, distortion, and repetition.

A human body transforms to leave its origin, to fit in or stand out, constantly shifting between adaptation and resistance.
