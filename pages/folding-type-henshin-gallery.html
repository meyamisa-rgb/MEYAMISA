Project Description

The folding type Intramuros is an intensive one-week short course in which we explored the flexibility and folding abilities of typography, we focused on what we interpreted in folding something.

For the adaption, we could use any medium, from the obvious adaption like folding letters or photography film, collage and whatever came up in our mind.

By the end of the project, we needed to present it to our Professor and fellow students. My work content was mostly collage compositions of artwork and short film series.

Personal note:

A human body transforms to leave its origin, to fit in or stand out, it imitates folding movêments and characteristics.

This project is based on dance movements, transforming into collage artwork. It is something between art, design and the unknown.
