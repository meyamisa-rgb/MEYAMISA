Folding Type - Henshin Gallery

Supervision: Prof. Holger Jacobs

Project: Murosweek 2023

Videos

Movement studies extending the same body-language experiments in time.

Project Description

Henshin began as a one-week intensity inside Intramuros, where folding was treated as a design method, not just a visual effect. I tested how a body can behave like a letter: stretching, collapsing, repeating, and interrupting itself.

Instead of keeping typography fixed on a page, I translated it into movement, collage, and camera distortion. The resulting forms are unstable on purpose, caught between clarity and noise, precision and instinct.

The photo sequence and video studies work as one system: each frame is a temporary alphabet where posture becomes structure. The project sits between art, design, and choreography, exploring how type can be felt before it is read.
