About

Mey Amisa

Cuban-German interdisciplinary designer and artist based in Germany. Her practice combines experimental typography, visual storytelling, filmmaking, performance, and installation.

Her work explores how movement, language, and emotion can exist as one system. Through exhibitions in Germany, Japan, and South Korea, she develops visual worlds that connect body and text.

Languages

German (Native), Spanish (Native), English (C1), Japanese (N4-N3)

Focus

Experimental typography, exhibition design, visual writing systems, and body-led image research.

Education

Peter Behrens School of Arts, University of Applied Sciences Dusseldorf (B.A. Communication Design, 2020-2026)

Doshisha Women's College, Kyoto (Exchange Program, 2024)

Selected Exhibitions

Raststätte Kulturraum Aachen (2025), Geoje International Seomkkot Art Show (2025), Studio Kan Kyoto (2024), PUNIO Tokyo (2023-2024)
